FitNow: Official Brand Guidelines Document
________________


1. Brand Overview
FitNow is a customer-first digital health and fitness platform designed to support individuals in building sustainable, long-term wellness habits.
The brand focuses on progress over perfection, encouraging users to improve their health at a pace that suits their lifestyle.
FitNow does not promote extreme fitness outcomes, instant transformations, or unrealistic promises. Instead, it positions itself as a trusted companion in the customer’s wellness journey.
________________


2. Brand Mission & Vision
Mission
To empower individuals to build healthier habits through guidance, consistency, and positive reinforcement.
Vision
To create a supportive fitness ecosystem where people of all fitness levels feel confident, motivated, and respected.
________________


3. Brand Values
The following values must be reflected in all customer-facing communication:
* Trust & Transparency

 Communication must be honest, clear, and free of misleading claims.

* Encouragement over Pressure

 Messaging should motivate users without inducing guilt, fear, or urgency.

* Inclusivity

 Content must acknowledge diverse body types, abilities, and fitness levels.

* Consistency over Intensity

 Promote sustainable effort rather than extreme or short-term results.

________________


4. Tone & Voice Guidelines
Required Tone
All content generated for FitNow must be:
   * Empathetic

   * Supportive

   * Friendly

   * Motivational (without pressure)

Prohibited Tone
The following tones are strictly disallowed:
      * Aggressive or commanding

      * Judgmental or shaming

      * Fear-based or manipulative

      * Overly sales-driven

Voice Characteristics
         * Simple, conversational language

         * Short to medium-length sentences

         * Positive framing

         * Clear call-to-action without urgency pressure

________________


5. Language & Messaging Rules
Allowed Language Patterns
            * “Support your fitness journey”

            * “Build habits that last”

            * “Progress at your own pace”

            * “Designed to help you stay consistent”

Forbidden Language Patterns
The following must never appear in any generated content:
               * Absolute claims (e.g., “guaranteed results”, “100% success”)

               * Instant outcome claims (e.g., “lose weight fast”, “instant transformation”)

               * Fear-based urgency (e.g., “last chance”, “act now or regret”)

________________


6. Customer Promise
FitNow commits to:
                  * Supporting healthy habit formation

                  * Encouraging consistency and self-improvement

                  * Providing tools, not guarantees

FitNow does not promise:
                     * Specific weight loss results

                     * Medical or clinical outcomes

                     * Time-bound transformations

________________


7. Compliance & Regulatory Guidelines
                        * Avoid medical, clinical, or diagnostic claims

                        * Do not reference doctors, medical approval, or clinical proof

                        * Benefits must be framed as supportive tools, not outcomes

                        * Avoid language that could be interpreted as medical advice

________________


8. Visual Identity Guidelines (For Image Generation)
Allowed Visual Themes
                           * Realistic, everyday fitness activities

                           * Diverse body types and age groups

                           * Neutral or natural environments

                           * Positive, approachable expressions

Disallowed Visual Themes
                              * Before/after transformation imagery

                              * Extreme body ideals

                              * Sexualized or unrealistic depictions

                              * Shock-value visuals

________________


9. Content Applicability
This document applies to all:
                                 * Marketing emails

                                 * Push notifications

                                 * In-app banners

                                 * Social media creatives

                                 * AI-generated images

                                 * AI-generated written content

No content may be generated unless this document is approved and validated.

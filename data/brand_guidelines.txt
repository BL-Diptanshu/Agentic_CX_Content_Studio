# CyberNova Brand Guidelines

## Brand Overview
CyberNova is a cutting-edge technology company specializing in AI-powered consumer electronics and smart devices. Our mission is to make advanced technology accessible, intuitive, and delightful for everyone.

## Brand Voice & Tone

### Voice Attributes
- **Innovative**: We speak about the future with confidence and excitement
- **Approachable**: Complex technology explained in simple, friendly terms
- **Empowering**: We help users unlock their potential
- **Authentic**: Honest about capabilities and limitations

### Tone Guidelines
- **Marketing Materials**: Enthusiastic, inspiring, future-focused
- **Product Descriptions**: Clear, benefit-driven, educational
- **Social Media**: Conversational, witty, engaging
- **Customer Support**: Patient, helpful, solution-oriented

## Visual Identity

### Color Palette
**Primary Colors**:
- Electric Blue (#0066FF) - Innovation and trust
- Neon Purple (#9D4EDD) - Creativity and premium quality
- Deep Space (#0A0E27) - Sophistication and depth

**Secondary Colors**:
- Cyber Cyan (#00F5FF) - Energy and connectivity
- Silver (#C0C0C0) - Precision and technology
- White (#FFFFFF) - Clarity and simplicity

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Outfit Bold (Modern, geometric)
- **Body Text**: Inter Regular (Clear, readable)
- **Accent**: Space Mono (Tech-forward, distinctive)

## Target Audience

### Primary Segments

**Tech Enthusiasts (25-40)**
- Early adopters of new technology
- Value innovation and performance
- Active on tech forums and social media
- Price-conscious but willing to pay for quality

**Creative Professionals (28-45)**
- Content creators, designers, photographers
- Need powerful, reliable tools
- Appreciate aesthetic design
- Value seamless workflow integration

**Young Professionals (22-35)**
- Career-focused individuals
- Seek productivity and efficiency
- Style-conscious consumers
- Mobile-first users

## Content Guidelines

### Writing Style
- Use active voice over passive
- Keep sentences concise (max 20 words)
- Avoid technical jargon unless necessary
- Lead with benefits, not features
- Use storytelling to create emotional connections

### Prohibited Language
- Avoid superlatives without evidence (best, perfect, revolutionary)
- No fear-based messaging
- Don't disparage competitors
- Avoid gender-biased language

### Social Media Best Practices
- **Instagram**: High-quality product photography, lifestyle shots, user content
- **Twitter/X**: Tech news, product updates, customer engagement
- **LinkedIn**: Industry insights, company culture, innovation stories
- **TikTok**: Behind-the-scenes, product demos, trending challenges

## Campaign Examples

**Pre-Launch**:

## Brand Do's and Don'ts

### Do's
✓ Highlight real user benefits
✓ Use authentic customer stories
✓ Show diverse representation in imagery
✓ Maintain consistent visual identity
✓ Respond promptly to customer feedback
✓ Celebrate community achievements

### Don'ts
✗ Make promises we can't keep
✗ Use stock photos when custom is possible
✗ Rely solely on technical specifications
✗ Ignore negative feedback
✗ Copy competitor messaging
✗ Sacrifice clarity for creativity

## Legal & Compliance

### Trademark Usage
- Always use ® symbol with CyberNova brand name
- Product names use ™ until registered
- Never modify logo or brand colors

### Claims Requirements
- Performance claims must be verified
- Include necessary disclaimers (battery life, AI features)
- Regional variations for regulatory compliance

### Privacy Messaging
- Be transparent about data collection
- Highlight user control over privacy settings
- Comply with GDPR, CCPA, and local regulations
